Haeyoom Website Design & Development - John Nalzaro

Project Overview

Designed and developed a custom website for Haeyoom, a Korean hair salon, featuring tailored hover effects, a functional search bar, and custom typography implemented through code.

Typography and Colour Strategy

Colour Strategy - The website follows Haeyoom's brand aesthetic, using grey, black, and white for a clean, modern, and sophisticated look.

Typography Pairing - For balanced and elegant layout, I used Poppins for headings, offering a contemporary and bold feel, while Noto Sans KR was used for paragraph text to ensure readability and seamless integration of Korean characters.
